Experience Kashmir- A Family Getaway Overview


A vacation to this paradise on earth, Kashmir, is an epic expedition through the Himalayas away from routine life, soul filled adventures, and the ridiculously pretty Dal Lake that will rejuvenate the family bond like never before. Sail through the unique lifestyle of the Dal Lake, where the Shikaras meander, the lavish houseboats lie still, and the colorful markets float over the rippling water.

Top 5 Attractions in the Destination

  1. Dal Lake
  2. Culture of Mir Bahris
  3. Gadsar Lake
  4. Aru Valley
  5. Majestic Himalayas

Must-try foods in the Destination

  • Kashmiri Wazwan
  • Dum Aloo
  • Rogan Josh
  • Yakhni
  • Kawa Tea
